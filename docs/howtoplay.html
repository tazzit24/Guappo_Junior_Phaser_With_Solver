Guappo Junior Game Rules

Objective:
Guide all your cosmonaut bear friends to the honeycombs at the same time, avoiding traps and enemies.

Turn Sequence: Guappo, Friends, and Enemies

Each turn in Guappo Junior is divided into two main phases, and within the player phase, Guappo (the main hero) always moves first, followed by the other friends:

  1. Player Phase (Guappo & Friends):
    • You choose a direction (up, down, left, right) for all your friends (including Guappo) to move.
    • Guappo (the main hero) always moves first. Then, each friend moves in order.
    • If a friend cannot move (blocked by a wall, obstacle, or another friend), they stay in place for that turn.
    • If a friend steps onto a trap or into an enemy, the level is lost immediately.
    • If all friends reach their honeycomb at the same time, the level is won immediately.
  2. Enemy Phase:
    • After all friends have moved, enemies (like bees) take their turn.
    • Each enemy moves according to its own pattern (e.g., horizontal, vertical, or other scripted paths).
    • If an enemy moves onto a tile occupied by a bear, the level is lost immediately.

Important:
- The order of movement is always: all friends move first, then all enemies move.
- The puzzle is to find a sequence of moves that gets all friends to the goal without anyone being caught or left behind.

Detailed Rules:

  1. Synchronize Your Team:
    • All friends must reach their destination (the honeycombs) together. If even one is left behind, you don't win.
  2. Turn-Based Movement:
    • Each turn, you plan and execute moves for all bears. After your move, enemies (like bees) take their turn.
  3. Avoid Hazards:
    • The map is filled with traps and moving enemies. If any bear is caught by an enemy or falls into a trap, you lose the level.
  4. Puzzle Logic:
    • Each level is a puzzle. You must find the right sequence of moves to get everyone to the goal safely and in sync.
  5. Scoring:
    • The fewer moves you use, the higher your score. Try to solve each level as efficiently as possible.
  6. Replay and Experiment:
    • You can retry levels as many times as you want. Experiment with different strategies to find the optimal solution.

Summary:

Plan carefully, synchronize your team, dodge hazards, and reach the goal together!
