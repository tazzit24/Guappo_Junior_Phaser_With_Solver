How to Play

Objective

Guide Guappo and his Friends to the Honeycombs. All bears must reach a honeycomb at the same time to win the level.

Game Elements

Guappo (Hero)

The main character. Moves 1 step per turn. You control his direction.

Slow Friend

Guappo's bear friends. They move 1 step per turn in the same direction as Guappo.

Fast Friend

Some of Guappo's friends move 2 steps per turn in the chosen direction. They can reach goals or danger faster, so plan carefully!

Beehive (Goal)

The destination. Every bear must be standing on a beehive to complete the level.

Gap

A hole in the ground. Bears cannot walk over gaps; they act like walls. Use them to rearrange heroes strategically.

Enemies

Enemies move after your turn. Avoid them at all costs!

Horizontal Bee (Slow)

Moves 1 step left or right. Bounces off walls.

Vertical Bee (Slow)

Moves 1 step up or down. Bounces off walls.

Diagonal Bee (Slow)

Moves 1 step diagonally. Bounces off walls.

Horizontal Bee (Fast)

Moves 2 steps per turn.

Vertical Bee (Fast)

Moves 2 steps per turn.

Diagonal Bee (Fast)

Moves 2 steps per turn.

Trap

Deadly! If any bear steps on a trap, the level is lost.

Rules & Mechanics

  • Synchronized Movement: When you swipe or press a key, ALL bears move in that direction.
  • Turn Order:
    1. Guappo moves.
    2. Friends move.
    3. Enemies move.
  • Blocking: Bears can block each other. Use this to protect friends or keep them in sync!
  • Winning: The level ends only when ALL bears are safe in a beehive.
